Eye-Care LED

Flicker-free, glare-free, medical-grade LEDs minimize eye strain through long sessions.

App Control

Modes, timers and schedules right from your phone — and a smooth integration with voice assistants.

OUR STORY

Light shapes time,
not just space

Lumio began with a belief — that light is not a tool, but a feeling. We sit at the same desk every day, but those hours are never the same. Morning focus, afternoon meetings, midnight thought — each deserves its own light.

Three years of work between engineers and designers led us to a lamp that reaches as close to human emotion as light can.
